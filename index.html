Course Structure

The course is structured over a span of 4 weeks. Each week focuses on different aspects of yoga for your overall well-being.

Week 1: Foundation and Breathing

  • Introduction to Yoga and its Benefits
  • Basic Asanas (Postures)
  • Breathing Techniques (Pranayama)
  • Stretching and Flexibility
Diet Plan for Week 1:
Vegetarian Option:
